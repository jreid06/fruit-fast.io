Fruit Combining Rules

✓ Pair Together
  • Sweet fruits + sub-acid fruits
  • Sub-acid + acid fruits
  • Melons alone (always)
  • Citrus + berries
  • Stone fruits together
  • Banana + dates (small amounts)
✕ Never Mix
  • Melons with any other fruit
  • Sweet fruits + acid fruits
  • Banana + citrus
  • Dates + acid fruits
  • High sugar + high water fruits
  • Too many varieties at once (3 max)
💧 Daily Hydration Rule Drink 2–2.5L of water daily, always 30 min before or 60 min after eating. Herbal teas (peppermint, ginger) are allowed. Avoid drinking during or directly after meals — it dilutes digestion.

General Tips

  • ⏰Leave at least 2.5–3 hours between meals for full digestion before the next portion.
  • 🌡️Eat fruit at room temperature where possible — cold fruit slows digestion.
  • 🦷Rinse your mouth with water after citrus to protect tooth enamel.
  • 😴Stop eating by 8:00 PM to give your body a 12-hour overnight fast.
  • 🤢Feeling nauseous or dizzy is normal on day 1–2. Rest, hydrate, and push through.
  • 🔁Breaking the fast: on day 4, reintroduce food with a light salad, not a heavy meal.
